## About the Agency

The Agency for Care Effectiveness (ACE) was established by the Ministry of Health (Singapore) to drive better decision-making in healthcare by conducting health technology assessments (HTA), publishing healthcare guidance and providing education. ACE develops ACE Clinical Guidance (ACGs) to inform specific areas of clinical practice. ACGs are usually reviewed around five years after publication, or earlier, if new evidence emerges that requires substantive changes to the recommendations.
